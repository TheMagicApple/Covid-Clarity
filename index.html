About
Covid Clarity is a statistics-based prediction app that gives you the probability that you have COVID-19. It takes in the locations you've been to and uses real formulas and math to calculate how many infected people you've come in contact with and how likely it is that one of these people gave you COVID-19.
Real statistics -> Real probability!
